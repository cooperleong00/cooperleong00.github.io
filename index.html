I am a second-year PhD student at Department of Computing, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU), advised by Prof. Maggie Wenjie Li. Before that, I received my BEng degrees from the School of Computer Science, Wuhan University, in 2022.

Research

I have broad interests in Natural Language Processing (NLP). Especially, I focus on the following directions:

  • Mechanistic Interpretability (MI). I am pursuing the interpretability of NLP models from a mechanistic perspective, which aims to understand the model's internal mechanisms of the model's behaviors.
  • MI for AI Alignment. I am interested in applying MI to AI Alignment, such as inspecting the alignment efficacy of an LLM, identifying misaligned outputs, and elucidating why it yields such outputs using MI.
